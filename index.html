Nevada

Nevada, a state located in the southwestern United States, borders Oregon and Idaho to the north, Utah to the east, Arizona to the southeast, and California to the west. Carson City, located in the western part of the state, serves as the capital. The most populous and well known city in the state is Las Vegas, which is found in the southernmost portion of the state. The total population of Nevada is about 3.3 million as of 2024, which ranks 32nd out of 50 states.
