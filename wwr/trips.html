Riverhawk Rapids

Riverhawk Rapids promises an unforgettable whitewater rafting adventure. A thrilling blend of adrenaline and natural beauty. As you navigate downstream, your inflatable raft dances through frothy rapids, challenging your teamwork and paddling skills. This run is perfect for beginners who are ready to take the next step into something a little more adventurous.

Spotted Rock

Picture heart-pounding drops, waves crashing around you, and the untouched wilderness enveloping the riverbanks. Whether you’re a first-timer seeking camaraderie or an experienced rafter craving the rush, the run at Spotted Rock delivers. So grab your paddle, embrace the spray, and let the river carve memories that'll last a lifetime! Spotted Rock is an exciting run for adventurers of all skill levels.

Turtleback Ridge

Perfect for beginners and families. Turtleback Ridge invites you to board an inflatable raft and surrender to the river’s whims. As your team of paddlers works in unison, you’ll conquer rapids—some playful, others fierce. Picture bouncing through waves, maneuvering around rocks, and feeling the spray on your face. Whether you’re a novice seeking gentle floats or an experienced rafter craving the wild, Turtleback Ridge caters to all.
