Whitewater Rafting Adventures has been in business since 1976 and remains one of Arizona's favorite rafting companies!! We are a small family run business that caters to making your trip a most memorable experience. Our unparalleled service, attention to detail, high end equipment and veteran guides make us leaders in our industry.
